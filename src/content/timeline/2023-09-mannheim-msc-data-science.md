<div class='achievement'>
  🎓 Thesis <strong>Probabilistic LTSF: Investigating a DMS-IMS Trade-off</strong>
  <p>Studied probabilistic extensions of point-based long-term time series forecasting models. While also introducing multi-world scenarios to evaluate temporal coherence in probabilistic forecasts. Results reveal a trade-off between accuracy and realism: while direct decoding yields strong marginal forecasts, iterative approaches better capture consistent future trajectories.</p>
</div>
